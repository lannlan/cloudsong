About Me

Hi! I'm Ranya Zhang, and I'm passionate about the intersection of data science, computer science, digital art, and traditional music. My multimedia work blends ancient sound with modern expression. The guzheng, a Chinese zither with over 2,000 years of history, is at the heart of this project. I combine it with real-time data and visual storytelling to create interactive experiences.This project is a tribute to the guzheng's beauty and versatility, showcasing how traditional music can be reimagined in the digital age. I hope you enjoy exploring this fusion of art and technology!
